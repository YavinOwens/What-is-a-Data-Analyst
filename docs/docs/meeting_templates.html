How to Use These Templates

  1. Copy the template markdown for the appropriate meeting type.
  2. Replace all placeholders (text within [brackets]) with meeting-specific information.
  3. Distribute the agenda to all participants before the meeting.
  4. Take notes during the meeting.
  5. After the meeting, update the template with notes and distribute to all participants.
  6. Store in the project documentation repository for future reference.

These templates are designed to be flexible and can be adapted to fit the specific needs of your meetings.

## How to Use These Templates

1. Copy the template markdown for the appropriate meeting type.
2. Replace all placeholders (text within `[brackets]`) with meeting-specific information.
3. Distribute the agenda to all participants before the meeting.
4. Take notes during the meeting.
5. After the meeting, update the template with notes and distribute to all participants.
6. Store in the project documentation repository for future reference.

These templates are designed to be flexible and can be adapted to fit the specific needs of your meetings.
